The Quiet Return of the Local Model

By Staff Writer · June 28, 2026 · 6 min read

For years the assumption held that serious AI had to live in the cloud. That assumption is quietly eroding as capable models shrink to fit the machines already on our desks.

What changed was not one breakthrough but the accumulation of many: better quantization, faster runtimes, and a wave of small models trained for narrow, useful jobs.
